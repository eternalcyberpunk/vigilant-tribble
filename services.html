Good to know

Our policies.

Arrive 15 minutes early

Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. If you arrive late, your session will be shortened but the full service fee still applies.

Credit card reservation

All services require a credit card on file to secure your appointment. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, and debit cards. No checks.

Cancellations

Please give us at least 24 hours notice. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged at 50% of service price. No-shows are charged the full amount.

Packages

Package pricing is a commitment to yourself — all packages are non-refundable and non-transferable. They don't expire, so use them at your own pace.

Gift cards

Available in any amount. Gift cards are non-refundable, non-transferable, and expire 90 days from purchase. Call us to purchase by phone.

Cell phones

For the benefit of every client in the clinic, we ask that cell phones be silenced during your visit. If you need to take a call, please step outside briefly.

Ready to schedule

Start with a single session.

If you've never had a colonic before, we recommend starting with one session so you know what it feels like. You can always commit to a package after.
