What we believe

Our approach to cleansing.

By taking time to thoroughly cleanse with the right support, we help break poor food habits that lead to an acidic lifestyle. The result isn't just feeling better — it's restoring your body's natural balance.

01

Time, not urgency

Cleansing isn't a quick fix. We recommend six to ten visits over several weeks for first-time clients, with seasonal maintenance after. No pressure to schedule more than you're ready for.

02

Gentle, not aggressive

Our slow-fill technique and careful water-pressure monitoring (never more than 2 psi) prioritize comfort. A good colonic shouldn't feel like a procedure — it should feel like rest.

03

Whole-body, not isolated

We pair colonics with lymphatic work, infrared heat, and nutritional support. What happens in the colon is connected to everything else, and we treat it that way.

The equipment

State-of-the-art, and rigorous about it.

Our colon hydrotherapy system is a pressurized, fully closed unit — FDA-approved and designed around a triple-filtration setup that includes both carbon and UV filters. All tubing is single-use and disposable. Every component meets or exceeds industry standards for sanitation and safety.

Because the system is closed and filtered, every session is completely odorless and completely private.

Our certifications

Trained, tested, and recertified.

Every one of our colon hydrotherapists is certified by I-ACT — the International Association of Colon Therapists. I-ACT is the leading professional body in colon hydrotherapy, and its certification requires ongoing education, hands-on testing, and strict ethical guidelines.

Certification isn't a one-time credential. Our therapists recertify, attend continuing education, and participate in industry conferences to stay current with best practices and equipment standards.
